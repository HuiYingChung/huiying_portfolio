About

Clarity through design.

I'm a Graphic Information Technology student at Arizona State University, graduating Summer 2026. My focus is on the intersection of UX thinking and front-end craft — I care about how things look and how they work.

My process starts with understanding the user and the goal, then building from structure to detail — information architecture, usability checks, visual refinement. I iterate until it's right.

This site was built in collaboration with AI. I believe knowing how to work with AI tools is a genuine skill — and the design decisions here are mine.
